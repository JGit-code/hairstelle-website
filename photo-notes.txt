Hairstelle – Suggested Photo Library
====================================

Use this list as a guide for the photos you add into the `assets` folder to complete the luxury look.

Hero & Brand
- hero-main.jpg      – Tall, elegant portrait of a model wearing a signature Hairstelle wig against a simple background.
- hero-studio.jpg    – Clean, minimal photo of the studio space or styling chair.

Shop / Products
- wig-crown-aura.jpg – Close-up of the Crown Aura Silk Wig on a mannequin or model.
- wig-midnight.jpg   – Image for Midnight Muse Bob Unit.
- wig-regal.jpg      – Image for Regal Essence Curly Wig.
- bundles-velvet.jpg – Bundles laid out neatly for Velvet Luxe Wave Bundles.
- bundles-opulent.jpg – Straight bundles for Opulent Tress Straight Bundles.
- frontal-ethereal.jpg – Close-up of the lace and hairline for Ethereal Melt HD Frontal.
- closure-couture.jpg – Image for Couture Seamless Closure.
- care-serum.jpg     – Product shot of Silk Serum Radiance Drops.
- care-heat-mist.jpg – Product shot of Crown Guard Heat Shield Mist.

Services & Studio
- service-silk-press.jpg – Before/after or side profile of a silk press.
- service-wig-install.jpg – Wig install in progress or final look.
- service-bridal.jpg – Bridal hair with veil or accessories.
- service-editorial.jpg – Editorial or runway style close-up.

Gallery / Clients
- gallery-client-1.jpg – Smiling client after install.
- gallery-client-2.jpg – Soft glam everyday look.
- gallery-bridal-1.jpg – Bridal crown moment.
- gallery-runway-1.jpg – Backstage runway shot.
- gallery-competition-1.jpg – Competition or trophy moment.

Brand & Details
- detail-logo-flat.png – Flat version of the Hairstelle logo for light backgrounds.
- detail-logo-dark.png – Logo version for dark backgrounds.
- detail-accessories.jpg – Hair accessories, combs, pins styled beautifully.

Admin / Behind-the-scenes (optional)
- admin-desk.jpg – Laptop, notebook and coffee for the admin dashboard illustration (optional).

Place these files in this `assets` folder and update the <img src=""> paths in your HTML if you want to replace the current gradient placeholders with real photos.
