About Hairstelle

Exclusive hair artistry from Newcastle

Estelle is an Exclusive Hair Artist and founder of Hairstelle, a private studio based in Newcastle, known for luxury styling, refined detail, and an award-winning journey from small-town dream to international stage.

Estelle’s story

From passionate hobby to world stage

What began as a passionate hobby grew into a purpose-led career shaped by faith, discipline, and a love for timeless beauty.

Estelle trained under world-renowned hairstylists whose work has featured in productions such as Game of Thrones, Harry Potter, James Bond films, Disney movies, and on global icons including Naomi Campbell, Jennifer Lopez, Nicole Kidman, and Miss South Africa titleholders.

In 2025, she was selected as one of only ten hairstylists to represent South Africa at the World Hairdressing Championships in Russia. Competing alongside 45 countries and more than 1,500 participants, she showcased her artistry in the advanced category of pastiche, creating intricate sculptures and garments entirely from hair.

Today, Hairstelle serves clients for birthdays, matric farewells, luxury bridal styling, special occasions, editorial moments, and selected travel-to-client bookings. Even the logo is inspired by a hairstyle Estelle created herself, reflecting her eye for detail and love for enduring elegance.

Guided by passion, artistry, and faith, Estelle takes pride in making every client feel confident and beautiful. Her journey is proof that when you love what you do and work tirelessly at it, dreams truly do come true.
